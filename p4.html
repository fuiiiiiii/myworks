THE BLACK HOLE MAGAZINE
graphic design
This is a magazine about Black Hole made for a Graphic Design course. It took about two months to develop from scratch to printing. Most of the photographs were taken or created by myself. The goal of this project was to use type, graphics, photos to express the theme that I have chosen, which is - The Black Hole.

Project Goal.

The goal of this project is to understand the complete process from designing to printing of making a magazine using grid, typeface, graphics and photographs.

Design Process.

  • Brain Storming
  • Research
  • Sketch
  • Explore Typography
  • Photography
  • Grid System
  • Graphic Design
  • Digital Printing
