User Story
Bonson is a software engineer who works at Apple in Cupertino. Ho is a board game lover and loves to social with other people. On a regular Thursday after work, he took a shower and decided that he wants to play few rounds of murder mystery board games but he does not want to spend time inviting people and go outside to play the game, so he takes out his iPhone and opens up Murder Mystery House app and taps on "Join a Room'. The game quickly matches players for him and after picking the story now he is ready to play with his new-friends-to-be!
Pain Points
1.Takes a lot of time to prepare.
2.Hard to match time with friends.
3.Hard to accommodate a place to host the game.
Needs
1.To host the game in an easier way.
2.To save money on buying scripts.
3.To social and meet new friends.
