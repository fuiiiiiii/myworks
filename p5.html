FINDING JELLIE
app design + coded prototype
Finding Jellie is an animal migration tracking app. It was one of my midterm project and my teammate and I built it from visual design to coded prototype for a made-up organization called Ani-Where. The animal we chose to work on is Lion’s Mane Jellyfish. My teammate is Viola Wang. We worked together for 3 weeks and it was an amazing experience for me to work with her on this coding project.

Project Goal.

The purpose of this project is to work in a collaborative environment to communicate and create an unique mobile web app.

Target Audience.
  • Primary: Lion’s Mane Jellyfish lover
  • Secondary: People who wants to learn more about this Jellyfish
  • Tertiary: Children who is learning about the Lion’s Mane Jellyfish
Function 1
Create a page to educate users about Lion’s Mane Jellyfish. The goal is to provide answers to the following questions: What are they? Why do they migrate? How does animal migration affect our environment?
Function 2
Create a page to provide Aquarium information of where people can see this beautiful creature in San Francisco.
Function 3
Help the scientific organization Ani-Where to track the migration of Lion’s Mane Jellyfish in order to analyze their living habits and patterns in different environment. Their goal is to find out how to protect the Lions Mane jellyfish.
