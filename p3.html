ABOUT

WECHAT
app redesign, user experience
WeChat is a Chinese multi-purpose messaging, social media and mobile payment app developed by Tencent. In order to provide better user experience in this app, I proposed to redesign 3 features in the app to optimize user experience.

Project Goal.

To address the principles of usable design such as listening to users, understanding the medium and the ability to bridge the two with a redesigned user interface. Objectively evaluate the usability of products, design from user requirements, and test designs to verify successes or uncover flaws

Redesign Goals.

1. Provide Mutual Friends Information: add a feature that shows mutual friend information between users on contact’s profile page.
2. Redesign The Qr Code Button: relocate san QR code button to the main chat page to enable efficiency.
3. Relocate Manage Discover Settings To Discover Page

Background Research.

  • WeChat-driven information consumption reached RMB 209. 7 billion.
  • WeChat drove RMB 333.9 billion traditional consumption.
  • WeChat contributed the employment of 20.3 million persons in 2017.

Interview Questions.

Audience
  • 1. What do you do for work? Where are you from?
  • 2. Do you use WeChat? If not, what instant messenger app do you use?
  • 3. Does your work involve using WeChat a lot? How?
  • 4. How was your experience with the options to add contacts?
  • 5. Do you want more privacy with WeChat?
  • 6. Do you want to know more information about your contacts?
  • 7. Was it easy for you to manager features in WeChat?
Expert
  • 1. May I ask, Where are you from originally? What is your occupation?
  • 2. Does your work involve using social apps? How often?
  • 3. Do you rely on high-technology products heavily? What kind?
  • 4. How much time do you spend on social in general every day?
  • 5. What do you like about WeChat? Dislike?
  • 6. How do you feel about the advertisements in Moments?
  • 7. What do you like about WeChat’s UI design? What do you dislike?

Interview Results.

  • 1. Audience like to use WeChat for both entertainment and work purposes, they use WeChat very frequently every day.
  • 2. Audience wants to know more information about their contacts, between contacts.
  • 3. Audience do not know that there are more features in WeChat that they can enable. Manage setting is difficult to find.
  • 4. There shouldn’t be too many features with the video call because WeChat already has lots of features, adding too many can be messy.
  • 5. There are many options to add a contact in WeChat, but QR Code is supposed to be the most convenient way.
  • 6. It takes too many steps to get to User’s QR code.
  • 7. It is uncomfortable to switch app once habit is formed.
  • 8. Anita likes to type username to add a contact because it is located at a convenience place.
  • 9. Audience do not know there are more features on Discover page which she can enable in setting.
  • 10. Shumin wants to know more information about her contacts, but doesn’t want to give them hers.
