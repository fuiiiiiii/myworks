02 Persona.

Benson Chang
Age: 28
Occupation: Software engineer
Board Game Lover
User Story
Benson is a software engineer who works at Apple in Cupertino. He is a board game lover and loves to social with other people. On a regular Thursday after work, he took a shower and decided that he wants to play few rounds of murder mystery board games but he does not want to spend time inviting people and go outside to play the game, so he takes out his iPhone and opens up Murder Mystery House app and taps on “Join a Room”. The game quickly matches players for him and after picking the story now he is ready to play with his new-friends-to-be!
Pain Points
1.Takes a lot of time to prepare.
2.Hard to match time with friends.
3.Hard to accommodate a place to host the game.
Needs
1.To host the game in an easier way.
2.To save money on buying scripts.
3.To social and meet new friends.

04 User Testing.

Paper Prototype
Testing Results:
  • 1. There is no “read story” function, players need to go back to story detail during the game.
  • 2. Texts and buttons are too small.
  • 3. Timer and clock are confusing to the tester.
  • 4. Setting button’s position is confusing to the tester.
  • 5. The tester cannot see cover story details.
  • 6. Pending indicator is not obvious enough for tester to notice.
  • 7. Too many information on Story Brief Page.
